Triage Overview

Triage is the rapid assessment phase of incident response designed to classify severity, understand scope, and prioritize immediate actions.

  • NIST SP 800-61 (Incident Handling Guide)

Interactive Triage Checklist









DFIR Triage Process (Expandable)

1. Validate Alert
Confirm alert source, verify indicators, dismiss false positives.
2. Identify Affected Assets
Determine which system, user, network, or application is impacted.
3. Assign Severity
Critical: Active threat, domain compromise, ransomware
Major: Contained threat, medium impact
Minor: Low risk or anomaly
4. Contain
Isolate host, disable account, block IP/domain, revoke tokens.
5. Document
Record everything: timestamps, actions taken, artifacts collected.
