20 Real-World Cyber Critical Infrastructure Incidents

A curated list of major cybersecurity events impacting global critical infrastructure systems

These real-world incidents span the energy, healthcare, government, transportation, financial, water, and food supply sectors. They are widely referenced in DFIR, SOC, and cybersecurity training to demonstrate the real-world impact of cyberattacks on national and global critical infrastructure.

Colonial Pipeline (2021)

Ransomware attack disrupted the largest U.S. fuel pipeline, causing regional fuel shortages.

Stuxnet (2010)

First cyberattack to cause physical damage, targeting Iranian nuclear centrifuges.

Ukraine Power Grid Attacks (2015–2016)

Nation-state cyberattacks caused widespread electricity outages.

WannaCry (2017)

Ransomware crippled hospital systems globally, including the UK NHS.

NotPetya (2017)

Massive global attack disrupted shipping, logistics, and manufacturing—Maersk severely hit.

Oldsmar Water Attack (2021)

Hacker attempted to poison water supply by altering chemical levels in a treatment plant.

JBS Food Supply Attack (2021)

Ransomware disrupted the world’s largest meat processor, threatening global food supply.

Sony Pictures Hack (2014)

Nation-state attack leaked terabytes of data and disrupted operations.

Norsk Hydro (2019)

Ransomware forced global aluminum production to revert to manual operations.

Baltimore City (2019)

City ransomware halted emergency services, billing systems, and city operations.

City Power Johannesburg (2019)

Ransomware affected electricity systems and customer billing.

Triton/Trisis (2017)

Malware targeted petrochemical plant safety controllers—first of its kind.

Ukraine Airport & Rail (2017)

NotPetya disrupted national airport systems and rail operations.

Industroyer/CrashOverride (2016)

Malware targeting electric grid automation systems—highly sophisticated.

Equifax Breach (2017)

150+ million identities compromised; major financial infrastructure breach.

SingHealth Attack (2018)

Singapore’s largest healthcare cyberattack; 1.5M patient records stolen.

SolarWinds Supply Chain (2020)

Nation-state compromise of software impacting governments and enterprises globally.

Costa Rica Gov Shutdown (2022)

National crisis triggered by ransomware impacting customs, tax, and healthcare systems.

Bolton NHS Ransomware (2022)

UK hospitals forced to divert ambulances; surgeries cancelled.

Medibank Cyberattack (2022)

Healthcare insurer breached; massive patient identity leak.
